The Gulf Fishing Club

This is a club for professional and hobbyists fishermen living in gulf countries. This website represents the club and allows its members to share fishing information, explore fishing spots and get to know the local fishing techniques. When you register, you will be given a member ID which will be useful if you take part in Fishing Tournaments later on. Members of GFC will have access to Offshore & Scuba diving packages any where in the gulf.
